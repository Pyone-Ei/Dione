Fire and Fire Love Compatiblity

Fire Signs- Leo, Aries, Sagittarius

When two Fire signs are together, they can turn life into a real adventure. More than this, they inspire and encourage one another. These two would spit Fire like dragons when arguing, only to makeup passionately afterwards. This is one of the strongest love connections as these two are drawn to each other and have so many things in common. They're both high-strung, impulsive and have a big ego, which can bring them many problems when it comes to relationships.

Water and Water Love Compatiblity

Water Signs- Pisces, Cancer, Scorpio

For better or worse, the relationship between two Water signs can be very deep. The partners can understand each other's needs and desires. Their desire to be in each other’s company comes to them naturally, not to mention they’re both very empathetic and caring. They're refreshing and at the same time deep, so their dreams are intense and their goals as a couple very ambitious. Problems may appear when neither of them would understand where their boundaries as a couple are.

Earth and Earth Love Compatiblity

Earth Signs- Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn

When two Earth sings are together, they make a power couple that can do anything because they would be both planning things to the last detail and make sure their future is bright. These two will always aim to improve each other and their relationship. Problems may appear when they're both focusing too much on the future and forget to live in the present. It's essential for both of them to agree on almost everything, even if there's also room for a little bit of friction from time to time.

Air and Air Love Compatiblity

Air Signs- Gemini, Libra, Aquarius

Two Air signs in the zodiac are very compatible with one another, seeing they can understand each other's logic and ways of thinking. However, this doesn't mean they can have a long-term relationship because they may have serious problems when their connection doesn't go so well. They must learn to talk about their emotions openly and listen to their intuition, as a couple. Their love can be just as intense as wind blows sometimes during storms but also peaceful and harmonious, when they have the same goal.

Water Signs- Pisces, Cancer, Scorpio

Fire and Water people can be very different from one another. While the ones born under the Fire element are passionate, the Water ones are very emotional. The relationship between these two elements can be very steamy if the circumstances are right. The Water sign helps the Fire sign be more emotional and instinctual, teaches him or her how to act with act and be nurturing.

Water and Earth Love Compatiblity

Water Signs- Pisces, Cancer, Scorpio
Earth Signs- Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn

The Earth can help Water put his or her imagination to work and ideas into practice. At the same time, the Water can teach Earth how to no longer be so rigid, just like it does when helping to re-shape clay in pottery. Their relationships are usually long lasting and their home a safe haven. Both partners are nice and respectful to one another, but also rely on facts when arguing and making decisions.

Water and Air Love Compatiblity

Water Signs- Pisces, Cancer, Scorpio
Air Signs- Gemini, Libra, Aquarius

Water and Air can benefit from one another because their relationship would be balanced from both an emotional and mental point of view. Though, the analytical and intellectual Air may not want to deal with all the drama Water has in store. Air can help Water be a thinker and communicate more efficiently. Air Signs- Gemini, Libra, Aquarius

These two elements touch but can't become one because the Air is all in his or her own head and thinks too much, while the Earth is only analyzing practicality and does't want to waste any time on thinking or working with his or her imagination.
