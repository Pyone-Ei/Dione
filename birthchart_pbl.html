What is Birth Chart (Natal Chart)?

An astrological birth chart, also referred to as the “natal chart” uses the exact date, time, and location of your birth to re-create a snapshot of the sky at your precise moment of arrival. A birth chart utilizes the placement of the planets and luminaries at that exact moment to create a blueprint that gives insight into aspects of an individual's personality, as well as pinpoints the timing of key life events. In this way, we can see that a birth chart is insightful, helping to guide us to be the best versions of ourselves, as well as illustrate the path forward.
